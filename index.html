FAQ

Good questions, honest answers

Is the skin analysis accurate?▾

GlowIQ uses computer vision to estimate skin characteristics from your selfie and gives consistent, comparable scores over time. It's designed to help you notice trends and track change. It is an estimate for cosmetic and informational use, not a clinical diagnosis. Lighting, camera and angle can affect results, so we recommend scanning in similar conditions each time.

Is my photo private?▾

Yes. Your selfie is processed privately for analysis and is never stored on our servers or sold. Cloud analysis is transient — once your scores are calculated, only those numeric scores are saved. Any progress photos you choose to keep stay on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I cancel my subscription?▾

Absolutely. You can cancel anytime from your Apple ID subscription settings on your iPhone. Your subscription stays active until the end of the current billing period, and it won't renew after you cancel.

Is GlowIQ a medical tool?▾

No. GlowIQ is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any condition. Scores and routines are estimates for cosmetic and informational purposes only. If you have a skin concern, a persistent issue, or are unsure about a product, please consult a qualified dermatologist or healthcare professional.
