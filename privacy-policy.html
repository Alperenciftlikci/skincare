Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GlowIQ ("GlowIQ," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the GlowIQ iOS app (the "App"). We built GlowIQ to be private by design. We want you to understand exactly what happens to your data — especially your skin photos.

The short version. Your skin selfie is analyzed privately and is never stored on our servers and never sold. Cloud analysis is transient — once your results are computed, only the numeric scores are saved. Any progress photos you keep stay on your device. You can delete your account and data at any time.

1. Information we collect

GlowIQ does not require you to create an account with a name or email to use the App. We collect the following:

  • Anonymous account identifier. We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to give your device a private, anonymous ID so your data can sync to you without us knowing who you are.
  • Skin scores and analysis results. The numeric scores and metrics derived from your scans (for example, overall skin score, hydration, texture, redness, pores).
  • App content you create. Your routine, scan history, notes, and preferences.
  • Subscription and purchase data. Managed through RevenueCat and Apple — for example, your subscription status, plan, transaction identifiers, and renewal/expiration dates. We do not receive or store your full payment card details; those are handled by Apple.
  • Usage and diagnostics. Analytics events and crash/diagnostic data to help us understand how the App is used and to fix bugs.
  • Device and technical data. Device type, operating system version, app version, and similar technical information.

2. How we handle your skin photos

This is the most important part of how GlowIQ works, so we want to be explicit:

  • Selfie analysis is transient. When you take a selfie for analysis, the image may be sent securely to our cloud analysis service to be processed in real time. It is used only to compute your skin scores and is not retained afterward.
  • We never store your selfie. Your analysis selfie is not saved to our servers, and we do not keep a copy of it after analysis completes.
  • We never sell your photos. We do not sell, rent, or trade your skin photos or any biometric-style imagery to anyone.
  • Only numeric scores are saved. After analysis, only the resulting numbers (your scores and metrics) are stored so you can see your trends over time.
  • Progress photos stay on-device. Any before-and-after progress photos you choose to keep are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers.

3. How we use information

  • To provide the core features: skin analysis, scores, personalized phased routines, and the product/ingredient scanner.
  • To track your skin trends over time and show your progress.
  • To manage and verify your subscription and access to premium features.
  • To improve the App, fix crashes, and understand aggregate usage.
  • To protect against abuse, fraud, and security threats.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

We do not use your skin photos or scores to serve you third-party advertising, and we do not sell your personal information.

4. Service providers we use

We rely on a small set of trusted third-party processors who handle data on our behalf under their own privacy and security commitments:

  • Google Firebase — Anonymous Authentication (anonymous account ID), Cloud Firestore (storing your scores, routine, and app content), Analytics (aggregate usage), Crashlytics (crash and diagnostic reports), and App Check (verifying requests come from the genuine app to prevent abuse).
  • RevenueCat — managing and validating your subscription and purchase data.
  • Apple — processing payments and managing subscriptions through the App Store.

These providers may process data in the United States and other countries. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) for international transfers.

5. Legal bases (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the App and the features you request.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure, maintain, and improve the App (balanced against your rights).
  • Consent — where required, for example for certain analytics; you can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation — where we must process data to comply with the law.

6. Your rights (EU / UK – GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@glowiq.app.

7. Your rights (California – CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what categories of personal information we collect and how we use it.
  • Request access to the specific personal information we have collected about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. To make a request, contact support@glowiq.app.

8. Account & data deletion

You can delete your account and associated data at any time, directly within the App (in Settings) or by emailing us at support@glowiq.app. When you delete your account, we remove your stored scores, routine, and app content from our systems. Progress photos stored on your device are removed when you delete them or uninstall the App. Some limited records may be retained where required by law (for example, transaction records).

9. Data retention

We keep your scores and app content for as long as your account is active so you can track your progress. Analysis selfies are not retained after processing. Crash and analytics data are retained for limited periods according to our providers' standard retention settings. When you delete your account, we delete your associated data as described above.

10. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit, restricted access, and Firebase App Check to help ensure requests come from the genuine app. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and limit what we collect in the first place.

11. Children's privacy

GlowIQ is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@glowiq.app and we will delete it.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App. Your continued use of GlowIQ after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
